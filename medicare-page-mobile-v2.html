No Preset Limits

How Many Sessions Does Medicare Cover?

Medicare Advantage plans have no preset annual limits on therapy sessions. Coverage is based on medical necessity as determined by your therapist's clinical documentation.

Think of therapy like dental care ‚Äì you don't stop brushing because a cavity healed. Many of our Medicare patients start with a specific challenge and stay because consistent support keeps them sharper, more resilient, and more connected.

Evidence-based approaches for older adults:

  • Cognitive Behavioral Therapy (CBT) adapted for seniors
  • Problem-Solving Therapy (PST) for daily challenges
  • Interpersonal Therapy (IPT) for relationship changes
  • Acceptance and Commitment Therapy (ACT) for values-based living
  • Mindfulness-Based Stress Reduction for age-related stressors

If you carry a Medigap plan, therapy costs are often predictable and very low. Plans B, C, and F cover everything ‚Äì $0 out of pocket. Plans D, G, M, and A require only the annual Part B deductible ($257 in 2026).

Plan G is currently the most common Medigap plan and provides excellent mental health coverage once the deductible is met.
